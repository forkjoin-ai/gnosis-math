# Buddhist Witnesses

[Parent](../README.md)

Lean witnesses extracted from Buddhist source documents. New Buddhist witnesses should be added in this directory and imported as `Gnosis.Witnesses.Buddhist.<Module>`.

## Witnesses

- `DhammapadaTwinMindWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 1, mind-first causality, hatred non-termination, truth/untruth discrimination, and practice over recitation.
- `DhammapadaEarnestIslandWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 2, earnestness as deathless path, flood-proof island, wakefulness, and fetter-burning reflection.
- `DhammapadaThoughtFortressWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 3, trembling thought tamed into a fortress; wrongly-directed mind as greater enemy and well-directed mind as greater helper.
- `DhammapadaFlowerBeeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 4, non-injuring extraction, scentless words without action, virtue traveling against the wind, and the lily from the rubbish heap.
- `DhammapadaFoolDelayedFruitWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 5, fool as false-ownership and delayed-fruit counterproof: evil ripens after latency, ascetic form is insufficient, and reputation desire splits from Nirvana.
- `DhammapadaWiseOtherShoreWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 6, wise correction, noble friendship, self-fashioning, lake-like serenity, and crossing the dominion of death toward the other shore.
- `DhammapadaArhatVoidPathWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 7, Arhat as finished journey, void/unconditioned freedom, subdued senses, quiet thought-word-deed, cut ties, and no new births.
- `DhammapadaThousandsMinimalProofWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 8, one meaningful word/day/moment outranking thousands, and self-conquest outranking external victory.
- `DhammapadaEvilAccumulationWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 9, delayed ripening, drop-by-drop accumulation, offense rebound, desire-free Nirvana, and no hiding place from deed or death.
- `DhammapadaNonHarmReboundWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 10, shared fear of punishment/death as non-harm basis, harsh speech rebound, outward ascetic marks failing without desire-overcoming, and self-fashioning.
- `DhammapadaOldAgeHouseBuilderWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 11, burning world and body decay, virtue outlasting chariots, and the house-builder passage where rafters/ridge-pole break and desires are extinguished.
- `DhammapadaSelfRefugeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 12, self-watchfulness, self as lord/refuge of self, self-begotten harm, self-purification, and the impossibility of another purifying the agent.
- `DhammapadaWorldMirageWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 13, world as bubble/mirage, sobriety brightening the world, lie-after-transgression as unbounded evil gap, and first holiness outranking world-lordship.
- `DhammapadaAwakenedRefugeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 14, trackless awakened one, compact awakened teaching, unsafe fear-refuges, and safe refuge through Four Truths and Eightfold Way.
- `DhammapadaHappinessNonOwnershipWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 15, happiness through non-hatred, non-greed, calling nothing one's own, giving up victory/defeat, rest, solitude, and wise company.
- `DhammapadaPleasureGriefFearWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 16, pleasure/affection/lust/love/greed as grief-fear generators, freedom from attachment, and upward-stream return.
- `DhammapadaAngerTripleControlWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 17, anger overcome by love, evil by good, greed by liberality, lying by truth, praise/blame instability, and body/tongue/mind control.
- `DhammapadaImpurityRootWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 18, impurity removed little by little, ignorance as greatest taint, vice digging its own root, own-fault attention, and outward acts failing to make a Samana.
- `DhammapadaJustClassifierWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 19, behavioral classifiers for just/learned/elder/Samana/Bhikshu/Muni/Ariya, with labels, violence, silence, tonsure, vows, and trance rejected as insufficient.
- `DhammapadaWayThreeRoadsWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 20, Eightfold Way, Four Truths, impermanence, grief/pain, unreal forms, self-effort, speech/mind/body as three roads of action, and cutting the forest of lust.
- `DhammapadaMiscWakefulnessWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 21, small pleasure traded for greater pleasure, body-watchfulness ending desire, sixfold wakefulness, and guarded symbolic-severance handling for the difficult Brahmana verses.
- `DhammapadaDownwardClassifierWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 22, false speech, unrestrained robe-wearing, careless practice, passion-dust scattering, frontier-fort self-guarding, and shame/fear/forbidden classifier inversion.
- `DhammapadaElephantTamingWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 23, elephant taming as self-taming: endurance under abuse, mind held like a furious elephant, solitude over fool company, and tamed self reaching the untrodden country.
- `DhammapadaThirstRootCutWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 24, thirst as creeper/root/channel/fetter/web, knowledge cutting the root, freed agents returning to bondage as counterproof, last-body closure, and law-gift over all gifts.
- `DhammapadaBhikshuEmptyBoatWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 25, total sense/body/speech/thought restraint, name-form non-identification, empty boat speed, mutual knowledge/meditation dependency, and quiet self-protected Bhikshu.
- `DhammapadaBrahmanaArhatClassifierWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammapada-muller.txt`, chapter 26, final Brahmana/Arhat classifier: desire-stream stopped, unmade understood, body/word/thought non-offense, birth/status rejected, fetters cut, no ownership, and end of births.
- `DhammacakkappavattanaWheelWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammacakkappavattana-sutta-thanissaro.txt`, lines 1-16, the first-turning wheel as exhaustion protocol: two extremes are rejected, the eightfold middle path opens vision and knowledge, the four truths close only after three rounds each, and awakening is not claimed until the 4x3 wheel is pure.
- `DhammacakkappavattanaPaliEnglishWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/dhammacakkappavattana-sutta-pali-english.pdf`, `pdftotext` lines 25-119, 122-235, and 239-333, cross-translation closure: introduction, English rendering, and Pali formula all converge on the same `tiparivaṭṭaṃ dvādasākāraṃ` wheel, with the deva echo ladder as distributed propagation.
