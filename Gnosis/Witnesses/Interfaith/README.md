# Interfaith Witnesses

[Parent](../README.md)

Lean witnesses extracted from interfaith and comparative source documents. New interfaith witnesses should be added in this directory and imported as `Gnosis.Witnesses.Interfaith.<Module>`.

## Witnesses

- `CrossTraditionAntiSubstitutionWitness`: Quran, Tao Te Ching, Gospel of Philip, and Tripartite Tractate convergence on anti-substitution: derived names, forms, copies, and interfaces remain useful only while they do not capture or impersonate the source. Bridges to `Gnosis.FailureAsStandingWave` by treating substitution claims as falsification-boundary nodes that standing-wave modes cannot support.
- `CrossTraditionProductiveVoidWitness`: Tao, Dhammapada, Gospel of Truth, and Quran convergence on productive void: emptiness, non-identification, deficiency, and hidden mercy function as use-sites rather than defects. Bridges to `Gnosis.FailureAsStandingWave` by treating void-misread claims as falsification-boundary nodes while a concrete standing-wave mode positively supports productive use-site claims.
- `ScripturesOfWorldCatalogOpeningWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/interfaith-scriptures-of-the-world.pdf`, pages 1-2, a bibliographic holdings ledger rather than a scripture body: title/author/location/publisher/ISBN/edition columns, Great Room convergence, translator/editor role metadata, missing identifier tolerance, multi-line extraction gaps, and alphabetic arrangement across traditions.
