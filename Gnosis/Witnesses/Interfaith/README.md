# Interfaith Witnesses

[Parent](../README.md)

Lean witnesses extracted from interfaith and comparative source documents. New interfaith witnesses should be added in this directory and imported as `Gnosis.Witnesses.Interfaith.<Module>`.

## Witnesses

- `CrossTraditionAntiSubstitutionWitness`: Quran, Tao Te Ching, Gospel of Philip, and Tripartite Tractate convergence on anti-substitution: derived names, forms, copies, and interfaces remain useful only while they do not capture or impersonate the source. Bridges to `Gnosis.FailureAsStandingWave` by treating substitution claims as falsification-boundary nodes that standing-wave modes cannot support.
- `CrossTraditionProductiveVoidWitness`: Tao, Dhammapada, Gospel of Truth, and Quran convergence on productive void: emptiness, non-identification, deficiency, and hidden mercy function as use-sites rather than defects. Bridges to `Gnosis.FailureAsStandingWave` by treating void-misread claims as falsification-boundary nodes while a concrete standing-wave mode positively supports productive use-site claims.
- `DiamperBadFoldCounterproofWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, formalizing the Synod of Diamper as a coercive bad fold: imposed hierarchy, oath/excommunication control, local assembly suppression, Syrian books amended or burnt, register-book destruction, Betti-hole recovery from surviving boundary traces, and an Orwell bridge where coercive retyping cannot rewrite the `2 + 2 = 4` arithmetic floor.
- `HospitalityTopologyWitness`: Greek myth and epistle convergence on hospitality as participation control: Philemon/Baucis constructive reception, Tantalus/Pelops child-offering as poisoned data, output-fed-back-as-input irreversibility, 2 John refusal of deceiver reception, and 3 John truth-worker support as co-labor with truth.
- `MalabarArchdeaconAssemblyResistanceWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, Angamale/Paru/Cochim resistance topology: Archdeacon as non-bishop continuity kernel, public oath instrument, armed consensus membrane, refusal of jurisdictional capture, and local ruler/regedor pressure as adversarial routing.
- `MalabarBodyLanguageWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, body-language topology from Cochim/Vaipicotta/Paru/Cheguree: kneeling and hand-kissing as courtesy without surrender, naked swords and closed doors as capture-risk signals, collective howling and signs for silence as consensus gestures, and absence/withdrawal as boundary control.
- `MalabarBookBurningArchiveHoleWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, Synod archive-damage layer: Syrian books compelled for review, offices and condemned books burnt, surviving books corrected, Roman-use replacements translated into old carriers, Malabar catechism inserted, and ritual/assembly/body-language/travelogue traces used for recovery around the archive hole.
- `MalabarChaldeanCarrierResistanceWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, pre-Diamper carrier-war slice: Latin/Jesuit education as failed interface capture, Chaldean/Syriac offices and patriarchal prayer as route memory, bishop seizure/interdiction after persuasion fails, and reordination/book-amendment pressure as sacramental retyping.
- `MalabarRitualMarkingSovereigntyWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/history-of-the-church-of-malabar-geddes.txt`, Vaipicotta/Paru ritual-interface slice: confirmation as bodily write operation, forehead/ear/beard/household touch boundaries, patriarch-prayer excommunication as route attack, and courtesy-to-foreign-bishop separated from jurisdictional surrender.
