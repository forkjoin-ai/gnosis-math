# Tao Witnesses

[Parent](../README.md)

Lean witnesses extracted from Taoist source documents. New Tao witnesses should be added in this directory and imported as `Gnosis.Witnesses.Tao.<Module>`.

## Witnesses

- `TaoTeChingNameMysteryWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 1-4, the named/unnamed Tao as a capture boundary: names teach by capacity but do not exhaust the enduring Tao; polarity co-arises; wu-wei and wordless instruction keep process from becoming ownership.
- `TaoTeChingBellowsWaterWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 5-10, non-possessive power as bellows, valley, water, last-place endurance, and mysterious quality: usefulness increases where self-claim and fullness are refused.
- `TaoTeChingVoidSensorWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 11-14, productive void and anti-sensory Tao: wheel, vessel, and room depend on empty use-sites, while sight/hearing/grasping fail and blend into the One.
- `TaoTeChingReturnRootWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 15-20, return-to-root as stillness protocol: muddy water clears by rest, hidden rule prevents wild movement, artificial virtue appears when Tao is lost, and the mother-source is preferred over public brightness.
- `TaoTeChingSourceCompletionWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 21-25, Tao as active-force source that evades sensory capture; completion arrives through partiality, crookedness, emptiness, and return, while many desires and self-display fail.
- `TaoTeChingTraceLessGovernanceWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 26-32, runtime governance as rooted stillness, trace-less skill, uncarved vessel-making, no-name rest, and sea-like reception of valley streams; kingdom-grasping and weapons serve as counterproofs.
- `TaoTeChingSoftReversalWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 33-40, the soft-reversal operator: self-overcoming, non-claiming production, hidden light, virtue-decay after Tao-loss, valleys full through void, and existence from non-existence.
- `TaoTeChingHiddenDiminishingWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 41-48, hidden and laughable Tao as high-value witness: Tao produces One, vacancy harmonizes all things, great fullness seems void, and practice advances by diminishing doing.
- `TaoTeChingReceptiveLifeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 49-51, receptive witness mind, no-place-of-death topology, and mysterious operation: produce and nourish without ownership, boasting, or control.
- `TaoTeChingMotherObservationWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 52-59, mother-source return, closed apertures, observation across scale, and anti-display governance where law display and meddling manufacture poverty, thieves, and distortion.
- `TaoTeChingLowPlaceWuWeiWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 60-69, low-place governance and wu-wei execution: great state as low stream, seas ruling by lower reception, early intervention before disorder, and gentleness as the only war-compatible victory condition.
- `TaoTeChingClosingCounterproofWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/tao-te-ching-legge.txt`, chapters 70-81, closing antitheorems: false knowing as disease, hard strength as death signature, water overcoming hardness, reconciliation residue, small-state sufficiency, sincere words over fine words, and non-striving closure.
