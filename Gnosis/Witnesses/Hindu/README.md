# Hindu Witnesses

[Parent](../README.md)

Lean witnesses extracted from Hindu source documents. New Hindu witnesses should be added in this directory and imported as `Gnosis.Witnesses.Hindu.<Module>`.

## Witnesses

- `GitaArjunaDistressWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 1, battlefield witness where kinship, duty, social order, and violence collide until Arjuna drops the bow.
- `GitaImperishableYogaWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 2, imperishable life, frame/body transience, equanimity across pairs, non-fruit action, sense withdrawal, and ocean-like stability.
- `GitaKarmaWheelWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 3, action as unavoidable, hypocritical renunciation as counterproof, sacrificial world-wheel, detached duty, qualities acting, and desire as wisdom's enemy.
- `GitaKnowledgeSacrificeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 4, ancient yoga renewed, divine descent when righteousness declines, action/inaction inversion, sacrifice forms, knowledge-fire, truth-ship, and doubt cut by wisdom.
- `GitaRenouncingFruitWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 5, Sankhya/Yoga convergence, lotus-like unstained action, nine-gate non-doership, unity vision across status/species, and sense-joys as grief wombs.
- `GitaSelfRestraintWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 6, self as friend/enemy by governance, moderation over extremes, lamp-sheltered meditation, repeated mind-return, all-in-Life-Soul vision, and failed right desire preserved.
- `GitaDiscernmentVeilWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 7, lower and higher nature, pearls-on-string dependence, three-quality veil, lower worship fruit, like/dislike snare, and death-hour holding.
- `GitaSupremeServiceWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 8, Brahma/Adhyatman/Karma classifiers, death-thought destination, OM meditation, no-return attainment, cosmic day/night cycles, and unmanifest endurance.
- `GitaKinglyMysteryWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 9, royal mystery, all contained yet source outside all, witness/refuge/friend/fountain/sea, Sat and Asat together, merit-heaven return, simple offering, and karma-bond release.
- `GitaPerfectionsCatalogueWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 10, distributed excellences as source-witnesses, wisdom-lamp, source as heart-seated spirit, seed, first/last/centre, and portions pointing to the source.
- `GitaUniversalFormWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 11, divine sight, manifold gathered in one field, time/death, already-held warriors, Arjuna as instrument rather than owner, and restored gentle form.
- `GitaFaithDevotionWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 12, embodied devotion as accessible path, unmanifest difficulty, practice/knowledge/meditation/renunciation ordering, and steady non-hating equal-minded devotion.
- `GitaFieldKnowerWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 13, field/knower split, humility and detachment as knowledge, Para-Brahm beyond Sat/Asat, Nature acting through qualities, and the same Lord in perishing forms.
- `GitaQualitiesSeparationWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 14, Sattva/Rajas/Tamas as binding classifiers, gunas as actors, equality across states and valuations, and devotion crossing the qualities.
- `GitaSupremeTreeWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 15, inverted tree, detachment axe, no-return place, spirit entering/leaving forms, divided/undivided/supreme levels, and Purushottama beyond both.
- `GitaDivineUndivineWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 16, divine and undivine trait classifiers, no-law/no-lord lust ontology as antitheorem, wealth-power delusion, and three hell-gates.
- `GitaThreefoldFaithWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 17, faith, food, sacrifice, austerity, speech, mind, and gifts classified by guna, motive, timing, recipient, and faith.
- `GitaDeliveranceRenunciationWitness`: `docs/ebooks/source-texts/bhagavad-gita-arnold.txt`, chapter 18, final synthesis: fruit renounced but duty retained, five causes, sole-doer error, guna classifiers, own-duty constraint, refuge, and Arjuna's delusion destroyed.
